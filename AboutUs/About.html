Meet the Team

Meet The Team

Our team is a diverse group of women who come together for one clear belief - that every person deserves a safe and stable home. Together we bring the same values to support families and individuals facing housing insecurity and are committed to help build that foundation.

Renee M.

Chief Excutive Officer, CEO

Renee comes from a background in Aviation and Unmanned Vehicle Systems Operations. She’s deeply passionate about making a difference in people’s lives, which inspired her to pursue a career where she can blend technology, problem-solving, and purpose. Renee enjoys using her knowledge to help others and contribute to meaningful, forward-thinking projects.


Imani M.

Chief Financial Officer, CFO

Imani comes from a background in banking and city work and is now building her career in tech. Her diverse experience has shown her how access, education, and opportunity can change lives. She’s passionate about using technology to give back and help her community especially by finding creative ways to support those facing housing insecurity and empower people with the tools they need to thrive.


Cynthia R.

Chief Communications Officer, CCO

Cynthia has a background in healthcare and technology, driven by a passion for helping others and creating lasting community impact. She believes everyone deserves a safe, stable place to call home and proudly supports the nonprofit's mission to end housing insecurity.


Zayra G.

Chief Operating Officer, COO

Zayra has a background in the veterinary field in helping those who are not able to speak for themselves. With her compassion and clear communication, she can make a difference in moments of crisis. She believes that everyone deserves a place of safety and dignity.
