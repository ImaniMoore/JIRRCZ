Frequently Asked Questions

Is CRIZ a nonprofit?

Yes, CRIZ is a transparent and reputable organization commited to using every donation responsibly to make a lastiing impact.

What is CRIZ’s mission?

Our mission is to combat housing insecurity by providing safe, stable, and affordable housing options for individuals and families in need. Beyond offering shelter, we connect people with resources such as financial assistance, temporary shelter, and long term support services to help them achieve lasting stability and independence.

I've heard some rumors about nonprofi executives making a lot of money. Does CRIZ leaders make millions?

We wish! As a team of apprentices, the only millions we’re handling are the ideas we brainstorm each week. No one at CRIZ is rolling in cash our focus (and our funds) go straight into helping people find stable housing Our leadership is compensated fairly and responsibly, following nonprofit standards and overseen by our volunteer board of directors. So, while we may not be millionaires, we’re definitely rich in purpose.

What areas does CRIZ serve?

CIRZ currently serves individuals and families experiencing housing insecurity in the Charlotte area. Our goal is to expand our reach to additional communities in the coming years as resources allow.

How can I support CRIZ’s mission?

There are many ways to get involved! You can donate funds, contribute household goods, volunteer your time, or become a community advocate for affordable housing. Every act of support helps us move closer to a world where everyone has a place to call home.
